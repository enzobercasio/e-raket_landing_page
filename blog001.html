Our Theory of Change

Our market is in a rapidly advancing digital world and having a digital profile or footprint has become increasingly important as so much of consumers’ attention are now focused online. Digital footprint is more crucial for most skilled workers and small businesses as most of the time, they lack the resources or has little to no knowledge in finding opportunities available in the digital market and are therefore unable to tap into the larger opportunities, attract more customers, build their portfolio and grow their livelihood or businesses.

For blue-collar workers or freelancers, especially in distressed areas, looking for their next ‘raket’ or side job in a digital world is a challenge. Looking for an additional source of income often means relying on personal networks and referrals, or by looking for or posting job and services along the streets. To add to that, with the recent events brought about by the pandemic, mobility is sometimes hindered due to travel restrictions and regulations.

On the consumer side of the market, more and more people are finding the importance of time and it has made them open up to the idea of paying someone else to do stuff for them while they focus on more profitable or enjoyable activities for them. Similar to the skilled workers or freelancers’ challenge, households and even businesses are finding it hard to look for people who can fulfill tasks or small jobs for them. Many would still rely on networks or referrals, trawling through Facebook pages or marketplace and advertising the requirement by physical job postings.

e-raket will be actively solving both of these common challenges on the worker and customer side. With our service marketplace platform, e-raket will help our skilled workers and small businesses find customers for their service(s). Households and businesses looking for people to fulfill their job requirement will be connected to qualified service providers. Our platform will be a great help in generating opportunities for these individuals and businesses, and at the same time create a marketplace for its customers.

e-raket

e-raket is a real-time, location-based job recommending platform. Our aim is to provide opportunities for additional income by complementing part-time or job requirements of the public and private sectors to a ready pool of qualified and skilled workers, freelancers and in the future, small businesses and service providers.
